Data collected:
- Account info (name, email, phone, gamertag)
- Profile photo
- Payment info (processed by Paystack, not stored in app)
- Device token for push notifications
- Usage analytics (Firebase Analytics)
- Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics)

Data NOT collected:
- Location data
- Contacts
- Browsing history

Third-party services:
- Supabase (database, auth, storage)
- Firebase (push notifications, analytics, crash reporting)
- Paystack (payment processing)
