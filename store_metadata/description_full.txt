CGE Lounge is the all-in-one app for Bonny Island's premier gaming community.

BOOK GAMING SESSIONS
Reserve your spot in the Main Lounge (PS5/Xbox), VIP Lounge (private setup), or VR Zone. Choose your game, pick a time, add drinks & snacks, and get a walk-in passcode.

MARKETPLACE
Buy, sell, and swap gaming gear with the community. Our swap-first marketplace makes trading easy — list your items, browse offers, and make deals with verified sellers.

ESPORTS TOURNAMENTS
Compete in weekly FIFA, Call of Duty, and Fortnite tournaments. Track your stats, climb the leaderboard, and win cash prizes.

COMMUNITY
Share your gaming moments, react to posts, join topic discussions, and connect with fellow gamers.

MESSAGES
Chat with buyers, sellers, and trading partners directly in the app. Marketplace listings link right to conversations.

MONTHLY GIVEAWAY
Earn entries by booking sessions, completing swaps, and winning tournaments. A new prize every month!

AI CONCIERGE
Get instant help finding games, booking sessions, or navigating the marketplace with our AI assistant.

FEATURES:
- Secure Paystack payments + pay at venue option
- Real-time notifications for bookings, swaps, and tournaments
- Seller trust system with ratings and reviews
- Full offline support — queue actions when offline, sync when back
- Dark mode gaming aesthetic
- Biometric authentication

Built for the CGE Lounge community in Bonny Island, Rivers State, Nigeria.
